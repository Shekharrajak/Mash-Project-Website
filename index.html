Our Vision

The vision of MASH Project is to build a community of technologically enabled social change makers.

Know More

What we do

We are building this community by focusing on three major areas .

  1. Developing cause based web and mobile applications
  2. Raising awareness through Social media and
  3. Organization engaging events like Open Dialogue, Hackathons, Workshops and Seminars.
